How to play

Memorize the tiles suggested by the computer and click the tiles following the past pattern, from memory, in the correct order, to advance to the next level.

Difficulty Modes

  • Change the game's difficulty mode by clicking the heading that says "MELODY". There are 3 modes: Easy, Hard and Pro.
  • Easy mode: black tiles only
  • Hard mode: white tiles only
  • Pro mode: black & white tiles

Score Calculation

  • Each successful level adds 100 points to your score. A wrong tile ends the game.

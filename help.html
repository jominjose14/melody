Help

How to play

Memorize the tiles suggested by the computer and click the tiles following the built-up pattern, in the correct order, to advance to the next level.

Difficulty Modes

  • Toggle between the game's difficulty modes by clicking the heading which reads MELODY. There are 3 modes: Easy, Hard and Pro.
  • The Easy mode involves just black tiles and the Hard mode involves only white tiles, but the Pro mode houses the full C octave from a piano, with 13 tiles, i.e, both black and white.

Score Calculation

Each successful level adds 100 points to your score. A wrong tile ends the game.

Pro Tips

  • First the obvious one: try using the melody created by the pattern to help you remember the tile sequence.
  • In case of repeated notes occuring together, try hitting them quickly every time to help your ear remember that they are repeated.
